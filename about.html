About TD Global Sports

TD global sports is a sports management Company founded to provide better guidance to elite athletes. We believe in a player’s talent, hard work and determination. That’s why we want to provide our athletes with the best opportunities possible, so they make it to the top. At TD global sports we scout for talented athletes, develop and market them. Each athlete has a dream that they are working tirelessly towards, investing their time and energy in that dream. This is where TD global steps in to build the athlete's dream and make it a reality. Respect, Trust, Honesty, and passion are the core of our business.

At TD global sports we work with student athletes who want to pursue a degree through sports scholarships. We provide them with the best possible university where they can enjoy the sport and earn a college degree.

Our Mission

To promote talented athletes across the Globe and managing them to unlock their full potential.

Our Vision

To revolutionise the sports industry into one were companies, teams, coaches Work together in building legacies for all athletes and have a positive impact in the community
